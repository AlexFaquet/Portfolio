Military Journey

A transformative chapter marked by grit, growth, and leadership — from the first footstep in Basic Military Training to leading as a Head Instructor.

Basic Military Training

I began my National Service journey with the Singapore Armed Forces at BMT. This phase instilled the fundamentals of discipline, resilience, and teamwork — values that became the foundation of everything that followed.

Military Working Dog Handler

Assigned to the Military Police K9 Unit, I underwent specialized training to work with working dogs. My bond with Nitro — my assigned partner — taught me lessons in patience, communication, and trust that no classroom could offer.

Head Instructor

I was entrusted with leading the Dog Handler Course — training new handlers and mentoring auxiliary instructors. The role honed my leadership, adaptability, and the ability to foster team cohesion under high responsibility.
